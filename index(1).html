Our Method

A precise approach to movement

Every session follows the same disciplined process. Assessment before adjustment — always.

01 — Assess

Measure, don't guess

We photograph and track shoulder, hip, and neck alignment from the front and side, comparing progress session by session.

02 — Align

Target the root cause

Pain and misalignment often live in different places. We identify the true source and focus each session there.

03 — Practice

A protocol you keep

After each session, we record your personalized movement protocol so the practice continues after you leave the studio.
